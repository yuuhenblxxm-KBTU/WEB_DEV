Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Fettuccine Pasta 400g
Heavy Cream 1 cup
Parmesan Cheese 1 cup (grated)
Garlic 2 cloves (minced)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic in butter.
  3. Add heavy cream and simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Mix in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Toss the pasta in the sauce and serve hot.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pizza Dough 1 (12-inch)
Tomato Sauce 1/2 cup
Fresh Mozzarella 200g (sliced)
Fresh Basil Leaves Handful

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the pizza dough.
  3. Add slices of mozzarella and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Dark Chocolate 200g
Butter 150g
Sugar 1 cup
Flour 1/2 cup
Eggs 3

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  3. Mix in sugar, then eggs one at a time.
  4. Fold in flour and pour into a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes and let cool before cutting.
